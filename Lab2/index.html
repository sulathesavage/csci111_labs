Atyrau (My Hometown)

Atyrau city is located in Western Part of Kazakhstan. Atyrau is also called "The Oil Capital of Kazakhstan" because the majority of oil and gas deposits are located in Atyrau Region.

Population

It has a population of 355,117 as of 2020. It is predominantly made up of Kazakhs, the minorities being Russians, Koreans, Tatars and Uzbeks.

History

Atyrau was founded in 1640 by the merchant Guryev. Guryev is the previous city name of Atyrau. Atyrau is a modern city with rapidly developing infrastructure and cultural life. It hosts major exhibitions and forums, cultural events, and social activities.
